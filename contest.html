October 11th & 12th at the Des Plaines Public Library

Contest

Want to show off your artist skills at the Des Arts celebration? Enter the Visual Art Competition for a chance to win!

Prospectus and Guidelines

Artists may enter one of three categories:

  • Adult (18 years old and older)
  • Teen (13 to 17 years old)
  • Youth (5 to 12 years old)

Sign Up

  • Starting September 18, sign up in person at the circulation desk of the library.
  • Only the first twenty applicants in each category will be accepted.
  • No telephone or email applications will be accepted.
  • There is no entry fee.

Eligibility

  • All entrants must be citizens of Des Plaines.
  • All 2-D work is eligible, including photography and digital art.
  • For the adult category, work must be framed and ready to hang with wire. Frame size may not exceed 16 x 20.
  • For the teen category, work on paper should be matted and mounted. Work on canvas and heavy pieces should be ready to hang with wire. Work should not exceed 16 x 20.
  • For the youth category, work must be matted, or stiff-backed or otherwise suitable to display on small easels. The work may not exceed 12 x 18.
  • The entry form must be completed and affixed to the back of the work.
  • Drop off the work at the circulation desk of the library on Tuesday, October 8 or Wednesday, October 9 anytime between 9am and 9pm.
  • Late entries cannot be considered.
  • Only one work per person will be accepted.

Awards and Prizes

  • Two prizes will be awarded per category.
  • The first award is Best in Show with a $100 prize.
  • The second award is People’s Choice with a $25 gift card to Starvin’ Artist prize.
  • Best in Show will be judged by preselected jury.
  • People’s Choice will be voted on by ballot by festival goers.
  • Winners will be announced on Saturday, October 12 at the library. All decisions about the awards will be final.
