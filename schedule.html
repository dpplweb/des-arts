Saturday, October 12, 2013 “Family Day”

Time Event Location
9:00 a.m. – 3:00 p.m. Art Art Show All artwork was submitted by Des Plaines residents. Make sure you vote for your favorite in each category. Winners will be announced at the Awards Ceremony following the Saturday performances.
  • 2nd Floor – Youth Entries
  • 3rd Floor – Teen and Adult Entries
9:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m. Performance An Historical Look at the Footlighters Theatre Company in Des Plaines Exhibit by the Des Plaines History Center. 1st Floor, Meeting Room Hall
10:00 a.m. – 2:00 p.m. Art Kids Community Arts Mural Be a Des Artist! Participate in painting a mural on the front window of the library. Drop by any time during the designated hours and let your creative spirit soar. Rhonda Popko of the Des Plaines Art Guild will supervise the project. Library Plaza
10:00 a.m. – 11:00 a.m. Art Kids Fairy Houses Build a whimsical fairy house using leaves, branches, acorns and more. Christy Piszkiewicz will show you how. 2nd Floor, Storytime Room
11:00 a.m. – 11:30 a.m. Performance Big Noise in Des Plaines Members of the Big Noise Theatre Company will give a short performance and describe volunteer opportunities available at the theatre. Big Noise performs at the Prairie Lakes Community Center in Des Plaines. 1st Floor, Meeting Room B/C
11:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m. Music Kids Pattisongs Singer/musician Patti Ecker will perform a selection of old-time songs using the mountain banjo and the parlor guitar. This interactive musical program is for children and parents. 2nd Floor, Alcove
11:30 a.m. – 12:30 p.m. Art Kids Mexican Tin Art Have fun creating a piece of Mexican folk art using metallic foils, markers and tin punching tools. Artist and teacher Russ Horvath will demonstrate the technique and help you as needed. September 15 – October 15 is National Hispanic Heritage Month 2nd Floor, Storytime Room
11:45 a.m. – 12:30 p.m. Dance Kids Dance Styles/Piano Solos Dance students from the AMA School of Dance & Music will perform three dance styles: hip hop, tap and ballet. Between the dance routines, enjoy two piano solos by music students. 1st Floor, Meeting Room B/C
12:00 p.m – 12:30 p.m. Performance Kids Storytelling Storyteller Reneé Ekman will share some fun dog stories with you. What funny stories can you tell about your pets? 2nd Floor, Alcove
1:00 p.m. – 2:00 p.m. Art Kids Scratchboard Drawing Make a scratchboard drawing to take home with you. Artist and teacher Russ Horvath will show you how to use techniques such as shading and cross hatching. 2nd Floor, Storytime Room
1:00 p.m. – 3:00 p.m. Art Picture Your Art Display your artwork on Des Plaines Memory, the online resource for Des Plaines history and culture. Bring digital pictures of your art to the Des Plaines Memory table and Digital Services librarian Steven Giese will post them online while you wait. 2nd Floor, Atrium Area
1:15 p.m. – 2:15 p.m. Dance Kids Artistry in Motion The Artistry in Motion dancers will perform routines in the contemporary, jazz and tap styles. A dance lesson follows the performance. Artistry in Motion Dance Company is a program of the Des Plaines Park District. 1st Floor, Meeting Room B/C
1:30 p.m. – 2:30 p.m. Music The Chordmasters Barbershop Quartet Listen to nostalgic music as the Chordmasters Barbershop Quartet strolls throughout the library. Strolling
2:30 p.m. – 2:45 p.m. Music Etude the Musical Get a sneak peek at Etude the Musical that premieres on October 16 at the historic Des Plaines Theatre. This original musical tells the tale of a legendary musician who lived 150 years ago in France. His life and accidental death are shrouded in mystery—perhaps because he did not die…he survives to this day as a vampire. 1st Floor, Meeting Room B/C
2:30 p.m. – 3:30 p.m. Art Kids Art Mirrors Design and color square wood tiles that will be used to frame a holiday themed art mirror. Ed Dougal of the Des Plaines Art Guild will show you how and offer help as needed. 2nd Floor, Storytime Room
3:00 p.m – 3:30 p.m. Performance Kids Storytelling Storyteller Reneé Ekman will share some fun dog stories with you. What funny stories can you tell about your pets? 2nd Floor, Alcove
3:00 p.m. – 4:00 p.m. Music Raining Sopranos Main Street Opera and Main Street Cabaret perform a collection of Opera arias and Broadway songs—reflecting on the many diverse and famous sopranos of today and yester-year. 1st Floor, Meeting Room B/C
4:15 p.m. – 4:45 p.m. Awards Ceremony & Closing Remarks 1st Floor, Meeting Room B/C
